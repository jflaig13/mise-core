## 1. Employee Roster

**Goal:** Populate employee roster for accurate name recognition in payroll transcripts.

### Questions:

**Servers:**

**Support Staff:**
- [ ] Who typically works utility/busser/expo roles?
- [ ] Do the same people rotate through different roles, or are they dedicated?

**Kitchen:**
- [ ] Any cooks who receive tip-outs?
- [ ] If yes, what names and what percentage/amount?

**Management:**
- [ ] Who besides you might be recording payroll?
- [ ] Do managers receive tips or tip-outs?

## 2. Shift Configuration

**Goal:** Understand shift times and structure for proper payroll parsing.

### Questions:

**AM Shifts:**
- [ ] What time does AM shift start and end?
- [ ] Example: "10:00 AM to 4:30 PM" (Papa Surf standard)
- [ ] Is it the same every day or different on weekends?

**PM Shifts:**
- [ ] What time does PM shift start and end?
- [ ] Does it vary by day of week?
- [ ] Example: "Sun-Thu 4:30-8:00, Fri-Sat 4:30-9:00"
- [ ] Are you on Daylight Saving Time schedule or Standard Time currently?

**Days Open:**
- [ ] What days of the week are you open?
- [ ] Any days you don't run certain shifts?

## 3. Tip Pooling & Business Rules

**Goal:** Configure correct tip calculation rules.

### Questions:

**Tip Pool Policy:**
- [ ] Do servers pool tips when multiple work the same shift?
- [ ] Or does each server keep their own tips?
- [ ] Any exceptions to the rule?

**Tipout Structure:**
- [ ] What percentage goes to utility staff? (Papa Surf uses 5%)
- [ ] Do you use expo + busser separately, or just "utility"?
  - If separate: Expo %? Busser %?
- [ ] Is tipout based on food sales or total sales?

**Special Cases:**
- [ ] How do you handle when support staff leaves early or arrives late?
- [ ] Do you prorate their tipout based on hours worked?
- [ ] Any other special tipout scenarios?
