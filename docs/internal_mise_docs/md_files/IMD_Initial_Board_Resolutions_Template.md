## ACTION BY UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT

The undersigned, being all of the members of the Board of Directors of **Mise, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "Corporation"), hereby adopt the following resolutions by unanimous written consent, effective as of:

**Effective Date:** /TextEffectiveDate/

---

## 1. ADOPTION OF CERTIFICATE OF INCORPORATION

**RESOLVED**, that the Certificate of Incorporation of the Corporation, as filed with the Secretary of State of Delaware on November 19, 2025, be and hereby is ratified and approved in all respects.

---

## 2. ADOPTION OF BYLAWS

**RESOLVED**, that the Bylaws attached hereto as **Exhibit A** be and hereby are adopted as the Bylaws of the Corporation.

---

## 3. ELECTION OF OFFICERS

**RESOLVED**, that the following persons are hereby elected to serve in the offices set forth opposite their respective names, to serve until their successors are duly elected and qualified or until their earlier resignation or removal:

| Office | Name |
|--------|------|
| President and Chief Executive Officer | Jonathan Flaig |
| Secretary and Treasurer | Austin Miett |

---

## 4. AUTHORIZATION OF ISSUANCE OF SHARES

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation is hereby authorized to issue **10,000,000 shares** of Common Stock, $0.0001 par value per share, as set forth in the Certificate of Incorporation.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to issue shares of Common Stock to the following persons in the amounts set forth below, in exchange for the consideration indicated:

| Name | Number of Shares | Consideration |
|------|------------------|---------------|
| Jonathan Flaig | 7,000,000 | Services rendered and IP assignment |
| Austin Miett | 3,000,000 | Services rendered and IP assignment |

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that upon receipt of such consideration, the shares so issued shall be validly issued, fully paid, and non-assessable.

---

## 5. STOCK CERTIFICATES

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation may, but shall not be required to, issue certificates for shares of its capital stock. The Board of Directors may provide by resolution that some or all of any class or series of stock shall be uncertificated shares.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to cause to be entered in the stock ledger of the Corporation the names and addresses of the stockholders, the number of shares held by each, and such other information as may be required by law.

---

## 6. ACCOUNTING YEAR

**RESOLVED**, that the fiscal year of the Corporation shall end on **December 31** of each year.

---

## 7. PRINCIPAL OFFICE

**RESOLVED**, that the principal office of the Corporation shall be located at:

## 8. BANK ACCOUNTS

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to open and maintain bank accounts with **Mercury Bank**, and such other financial institutions as they may determine from time to time, and to execute such documents as may be required by such institutions.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized to designate the persons who may sign checks and other instruments on behalf of the Corporation.

---

## 9. EMPLOYER IDENTIFICATION NUMBER

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation's Employer Identification Number (EIN) is **41-2726158**.

---

## 10. QUALIFICATION IN OTHER JURISDICTIONS

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to take such actions and execute such documents as may be necessary or appropriate to qualify the Corporation to do business in any state where such qualification is required.

---

## 11. INDEMNIFICATION

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation shall indemnify and hold harmless each director and officer of the Corporation to the fullest extent permitted by law, including advancement of expenses, as more fully set forth in the Bylaws of the Corporation.

---

## 12. RATIFICATION OF PRIOR ACTS

**RESOLVED**, that all acts and deeds heretofore done by the incorporators, directors, and officers of the Corporation in connection with the organization and initial operation of the Corporation are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

---

## 13. OMNIBUS RESOLUTION

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized and directed, in the name of and on behalf of the Corporation, to execute and deliver such documents, agreements, certificates, and instruments, and to take such other actions, as they may deem necessary or advisable to carry out the purposes and intent of the foregoing resolutions.
