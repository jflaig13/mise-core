## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship ("Purpose"). In connection with this Purpose, either party may disclose confidential information to the other.

## 2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party, including but not limited to: business plans, financial projections, product designs, technical specifications, customer data, pricing strategies, and proprietary software architecture.

Confidential Information does not include information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party
- Was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information
- Is disclosed with prior written approval

## 3. Obligations

The receiving party agrees to:

- Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent
- Use Confidential Information only for the Purpose stated above
- Limit access to employees or advisors with a need to know

## 4. Term

This Agreement remains in effect for **two (2) years** from the Effective Date.

## 5. Return of Information

Upon request, the receiving party will promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify such destruction in writing.

## 6. No License

No license or rights to any intellectual property are granted by this Agreement.

## 7. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of **Florida**.

## 8. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding confidentiality and supersedes all prior discussions.
