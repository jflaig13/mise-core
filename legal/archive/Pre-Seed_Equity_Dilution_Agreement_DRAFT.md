## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Founders currently hold equity in the Company in the following proportions:
- Jonathan Flaig: 60% of outstanding equity
- Austin Kelley: 40% of outstanding equity

WHEREAS, the Company intends to pursue pre-seed financing (the "Pre-Seed Financing");

WHEREAS, equity issued in connection with the Pre-Seed Financing will result in dilution of the Founders' ownership percentages;

WHEREAS, Austin Kelley has agreed that any dilution resulting from the Pre-Seed Financing shall come exclusively from his equity stake;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DILUTION TERMS

1.1 **Source of Dilution**
Any equity issued to investors in connection with the Pre-Seed Financing, up to a maximum of 10% of the Company's fully-diluted capitalization as of the Effective Date, shall be deemed to dilute exclusively Austin Kelley's equity stake.

1.2 **Maximum Dilution**
Austin Kelley agrees that his equity stake may be diluted from 40% to a minimum of 30% (representing a reduction of 25% of his current holdings, or 10% of the Company's total equity) as a result of the Pre-Seed Financing.

1.3 **Protection of Co-Founder's Equity**
Jonathan Flaig's equity stake of 60% shall remain unchanged and undiluted as a result of the Pre-Seed Financing, up to the dilution limits specified in Section 1.2.

1.4 **Resulting Ownership Structure**
Following the issuance of the maximum equity contemplated in Section 1.2, the ownership structure shall be approximately:
- Jonathan Flaig: 60% (unchanged)
- Austin Kelley: 30% (reduced from 40%)
- Pre-Seed Investors: 10%

---

## 2. MECHANICS

2.1 **Implementation**
The parties acknowledge that the actual mechanics of this arrangement may be implemented through one of the following methods, as determined by legal counsel and the Company's Board:
   a) Selective dilution of Austin Kelley's shares while protecting Jonathan Flaig's shares; or
   b) Pro-rata dilution followed by transfer of shares from Austin Kelley to Jonathan Flaig to restore Jonathan Flaig's percentage; or
   c) Such other method as may be legally permissible and tax-efficient

2.2 **Documentation**
The parties agree to execute any and all documents necessary to implement this Agreement, including but not limited to amendments to the Company's operating agreement, stock purchase agreements, and any other corporate documents.
