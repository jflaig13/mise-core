## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the Company is engaged in the business of developing and providing voice-first technology solutions for the hospitality industry;

**WHEREAS**, Assignor is a founder and /TextAssignorRole/ of the Company;

**WHEREAS**, Assignor has conceived, created, developed, or contributed to certain intellectual property that relates to the Company's business, either prior to the incorporation of the Company or during the course of Assignor's involvement with the Company;

**WHEREAS**, the parties desire to assign to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to such intellectual property;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Assigned IP"** means all Intellectual Property that:

> (a) relates to the Company's actual or anticipated business, research, or development;
>
> (b) was conceived, created, developed, or reduced to practice by Assignor, either alone or jointly with others, prior to the date of this Agreement; or
>
> (c) was conceived, created, developed, or reduced to practice by Assignor, either alone or jointly with others, during the course of Assignor's service to the Company.

**1.2 "Intellectual Property"** means any and all:

> (a) inventions, discoveries, improvements, ideas, concepts, and designs, whether or not patentable;
>
> (b) works of authorship, including software code, documentation, specifications, designs, graphics, and other written materials;
>
> (c) trade secrets, know-how, techniques, processes, methods, and formulas;
>
> (d) trademarks, service marks, trade names, logos, and domain names;
>
> (e) patents, patent applications, and patent rights;
>
> (f) copyrights and copyrightable works; and
>
> (g) all other intellectual property rights recognized in any jurisdiction.

---

## 2. ASSIGNMENT

**2.1 Assignment of Assigned IP.** Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP, including all intellectual property rights therein, throughout the world, in perpetuity.

**2.2 Moral Rights.** To the extent permitted by applicable law, Assignor hereby irrevocably waives and agrees never to assert any moral rights Assignor may have in the Assigned IP.

**2.3 Further Assurances.** Assignor agrees to execute and deliver such further documents and instruments, and to take such further actions, as the Company may reasonably request to effectuate and perfect the assignment contemplated by this Agreement, including:

> (a) executing patent applications, copyright registrations, and other filings;
>
> (b) cooperating in the prosecution and defense of any intellectual property rights; and
>
> (c) providing testimony and other assistance in any proceeding.

---

## 3. PRIOR INVENTIONS

**3.1 Disclosure.** Assignor has disclosed in **Exhibit A** all inventions, works of authorship, and other intellectual property that Assignor conceived, created, or developed prior to the date of this Agreement that relate to the Company's business.

**3.2 Excluded Inventions.** If Exhibit A lists any inventions that Assignor wishes to exclude from this Assignment, Assignor acknowledges and agrees that such exclusion is effective only if:

> (a) the invention was conceived, created, and reduced to practice entirely before Assignor's involvement with the Company;
>
> (b) the invention does not relate to the Company's actual or anticipated business;
>
> (c) the Company has approved such exclusion in writing.

**3.3 No Exhibit A.** If Exhibit A is not attached or is blank, Assignor represents that there are no inventions to disclose or exclude.

---

## 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Assignor represents and warrants that:

**4.1 Ownership.** Assignor is the sole owner of the Assigned IP, and the Assigned IP is free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and claims of any third party.

**4.2 No Conflicts.** The execution of this Agreement and the assignment of the Assigned IP do not violate any agreement, obligation, or duty that Assignor has to any third party.

**4.3 No Infringement.** To Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not infringe upon the intellectual property rights of any third party.

**4.4 Full Disclosure.** Assignor has made full and complete disclosure to the Company of all Assigned IP.

---

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Assignor acknowledges that the Assigned IP constitutes confidential and proprietary information of the Company. Assignor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the Assigned IP and not to disclose it to any third party without the Company's prior written consent.

---
