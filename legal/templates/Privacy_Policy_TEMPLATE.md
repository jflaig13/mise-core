## 5. VOICE DATA PRACTICES

Given the sensitive nature of voice data, we want to be transparent about our practices:

<div class="highlight-box">

**What We Record**
Only voice memos explicitly submitted by users through the Service. We do **NOT** continuously record or monitor audio.

**How We Process Voice Data**
1. Voice recordings are sent to third-party transcription services
2. Transcripts are processed by AI models to extract structured data
3. Results are presented to users for review and approval

**Third-Party Processing**
- **OpenAI** (Whisper transcription) — subject to OpenAI's privacy practices
- **Anthropic** (AI processing) — subject to Anthropic's privacy practices

We have data processing agreements with these providers.

</div>
