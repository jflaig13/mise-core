## Tax Considerations

### 83(b) Election - CRITICAL
Since shares are "fully vested" as of January 22, 2026, **both founders should file an 83(b) election with the IRS within 30 days** (by February 21, 2026).

**Why this matters:**
- Without 83(b): You pay income tax on equity value as it vests (potentially at IPO/exit valuations)
- With 83(b): You pay tax NOW (on minimal/zero value) and future gains are capital gains

**Action Required:**
1. Founders should consult a tax advisor IMMEDIATELY
2. File Form 83(b) with IRS within 30 days of January 22, 2026
3. Send copies to IRS, keep copy for records, send copy to company
