## 2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

Mise provides a **voice-first operating system for hospitality businesses**. The Service includes:

- **Voice Recording and Processing** — Recording, transcribing, and processing voice memos for operational purposes, including payroll and inventory workflows
- **Payroll Management** — Processing voice-reported shift data, calculating tips, wages, and tipouts, and generating payroll reports for review and approval
- **Inventory Management** — Processing voice-reported inventory counts, normalizing product data, matching to catalogs, and generating inventory reports

The Service is designed for use by authorized personnel of hospitality businesses ("**Users**") and is not intended for consumer or personal use.
