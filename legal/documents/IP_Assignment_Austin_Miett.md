## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the Company is engaged in the business of developing and providing voice-first technology solutions for the hospitality industry;

**WHEREAS**, Assignor is a founder and Secretary and Treasurer of the Company;

**WHEREAS**, Assignor has conceived, created, developed, or contributed to certain intellectual property that relates to the Company's business, including business plans, operational frameworks, corporate formation materials, and other contributions, both prior to the incorporation of the Company on November 19, 2025, and during the course of Assignor's continued service to the Company;

**WHEREAS**, the parties desire to assign to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to such intellectual property, to ensure that the Company holds clear, unencumbered ownership of all its intellectual property assets;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Assigned IP"** means all Intellectual Property that:

> (a) relates to the Company's actual or anticipated business, research, or development, including without limitation the voice-first hospitality operations platform known as "Mise";
>
> (b) was conceived, created, developed, or reduced to practice by Assignor, either alone or jointly with others, at any time prior to the date of this Agreement, including the period prior to the incorporation of the Company on November 19, 2025; or
>
> (c) is conceived, created, developed, or reduced to practice by Assignor, either alone or jointly with others, during the course of Assignor's service to the Company in any capacity.

**1.2 "Intellectual Property"** means any and all:

> (a) inventions, discoveries, improvements, ideas, concepts, and designs, whether or not patentable;
>
> (b) works of authorship, including without limitation software source code, object code, scripts, configuration files, build files, APIs, documentation, specifications, technical designs, user interface designs, graphics, and other written materials;
>
> (c) trade secrets, know-how, techniques, processes, methods, formulas, algorithms, data models, system architectures, and workflow specifications;
>
> (d) trademarks, service marks, trade names, logos, domain names, and associated goodwill;
>
> (e) patents, patent applications, and patent rights;
>
> (f) copyrights and copyrightable works;
>
> (g) databases, data structures, schemas, and compilations of data; and
>
> (h) all other intellectual property rights recognized in any jurisdiction worldwide.

---

## 2. ASSIGNMENT

**2.1 Assignment of Assigned IP.** Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP, including all intellectual property rights therein, throughout the world, in perpetuity. This assignment includes all rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement of the Assigned IP.

**2.2 Moral Rights.** To the extent permitted by applicable law, Assignor hereby irrevocably waives and agrees never to assert any moral rights Assignor may have in the Assigned IP, including rights of attribution and integrity.

**2.3 Further Assurances.** Assignor agrees to execute and deliver such further documents and instruments, and to take such further actions, as the Company may reasonably request to effectuate and perfect the assignment contemplated by this Agreement, including:

> (a) executing patent applications, copyright registrations, trademark applications, and other filings in any jurisdiction;
>
> (b) cooperating in the prosecution and defense of any intellectual property rights;
>
> (c) providing testimony and other assistance in any proceeding related to the Assigned IP; and
>
> (d) executing any instruments necessary to record the assignment with applicable governmental authorities.

**2.4 Power of Attorney.** Assignor hereby irrevocably appoints the Company and its officers as Assignor's attorney-in-fact, with full power of substitution, to execute, file, and prosecute any documents or applications, and to take any other actions, that may be necessary or desirable to perfect or enforce the Company's rights in the Assigned IP, in the event that Assignor is unavailable, unable, or unwilling to do so. This power of attorney is coupled with an interest and shall survive the disability or incapacity of Assignor.

---

## 3. PRIOR INVENTIONS

**3.1 Disclosure.** Assignor has disclosed in **Exhibit A** all inventions, works of authorship, and other intellectual property that Assignor conceived, created, or developed prior to the date of this Agreement that relate to the Company's business. All items listed in Exhibit A are included in the Assigned IP and are assigned to the Company pursuant to Section 2.1 of this Agreement.

**3.2 Excluded Inventions.** If Exhibit A lists any inventions that Assignor wishes to exclude from this Assignment, Assignor acknowledges and agrees that such exclusion is effective only if:

> (a) the invention was conceived, created, and reduced to practice entirely before Assignor's involvement with the Company;
>
> (b) the invention does not relate to the Company's actual or anticipated business; and
>
> (c) the Company has approved such exclusion in writing.

**3.3 No Exhibit A.** If Exhibit A is not attached or is blank, Assignor represents that there are no inventions to disclose or exclude, and all work product created by Assignor relating to the Company's business is assigned to the Company.

---

## 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Assignor represents and warrants that:

**4.1 Ownership.** Assignor is the sole owner of the Assigned IP, or, where the Assigned IP was created jointly with others, Assignor owns all rights necessary to make the assignments contemplated herein. The Assigned IP is free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, security interests, and claims of any third party.

**4.2 No Conflicts.** The execution of this Agreement and the assignment of the Assigned IP do not violate any agreement, obligation, or duty that Assignor has to any third party, including any former employer, client, or contractor.

**4.3 No Infringement.** To Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not infringe upon the intellectual property rights of any third party.

**4.4 Full Disclosure.** Assignor has made full and complete disclosure to the Company of all Assigned IP, and there are no material inventions, works, or intellectual property relating to the Company's business that Assignor has failed to disclose.

**4.5 No Prior Assignment.** Assignor has not previously assigned, transferred, licensed, or encumbered any of the Assigned IP to any third party.

**4.6 No Government Funding.** None of the Assigned IP was developed using government funding or under any government contract that would grant the government rights in the Assigned IP.

---

## 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Assignor acknowledges that the Assigned IP constitutes confidential and proprietary information of the Company. Assignor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the Assigned IP and not to disclose it to any third party without the Company's prior written consent, except as required by law or in the performance of Assignor's duties to the Company.

---

## 6. CONSIDERATION

In consideration for the assignment contemplated by this Agreement, Assignor acknowledges receipt of:

> (a) 3,000,000 shares of Common Stock of the Company; and
>
> (b) the opportunity to participate in the Company as a founder, Secretary and Treasurer, director, and stockholder.

Assignor acknowledges that the foregoing consideration is adequate and sufficient for the assignments and covenants contained herein.

## 7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the **State of Delaware**, without regard to its conflicts of law principles.

## 8. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written, relating to the assignment of intellectual property from Assignor to the Company, including Section 8 of the Shareholder Agreement of Mise, Inc. dated January 22, 2026.

---

## 9. AMENDMENTS

This Agreement may not be amended except by a written instrument signed by both parties.

---

## 10. COUNTERPARTS

This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, including by electronic signature, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.

---

## 11. SURVIVAL

The provisions of this Agreement, including the assignment in Section 2 and the representations and warranties in Section 4, shall survive any termination of Assignor's relationship with the Company, whether as an officer, director, employee, consultant, or in any other capacity.
